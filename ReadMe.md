\# K Means Clustering Model


# The file consist of three major projects with the basis of K means clustering algorithm

* Economies Clustering

\# Objective: Observing the change in positioning of economies based on their per capita spending and growth rates in three growth phases(timeline)



* Players Clustering (Covariance, Correlation, PCA)

\# Objective: Applying covariance, correlation for understanding the variables behavior against each other (+ -) and applying PCA for finding the optimal numbers of clusters 



* Customer Buying pattern Segmentation

\# Objective: Based on two variables dividing the customers into four quadrants for right marketing strategy and product positioning
